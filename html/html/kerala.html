Duration : 07 Days / 06 Nights
Places Covered : Cochin - Munnar - Periyar - Kumarakom - Alleppey - Cochin
 

Day 01 : Cochin


On arrival at Cochin Airport our Tour Executive welcomes you and assist you further transfer to the hotel. Later go for a sightseeing tour of Cochin visiting St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue, Mattancherry Dutch Palace and Chinese Fishing Nets. Overnight stay at Cochin.

Day 02 : Cochin - Munnar (140kms/4hrs)


Post breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Munnar, the beautiful hill station which was the favored summer resort of the erstwhile British rulers in the colonial days. Munnar is blessed with unending expanse of tea plantations - pristine valleys and mountains - exotic species of flora and fauna. Enroute visit Vallara and Cheeyapara waterfalls. On arrival at Munnar check into the resort and relax. Later go for sightseeing tour of Munnar visiting Raja Mala - the highest peak in Munnar, Eravikulam National Park where Nilgiri Thar (mountain goats) are found, Mattupetty dam. Overnight stay at Munnar.

Day 03 : Munnar


Post breakfast visit Devikulam a beautiful hill station with mineral spa, Pallivasal venue for first hydroelectric project in Kerala, Tata tea plantations and eco - point. Overnight stay at Munnar.

Day 04 : Munnar - Periyar (130kms/4hrs)


Post breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Periyar also known as Thekkady, a land full of cardamom and spice plantation and home to one of the largest wildlife reserves in India. On arrival check into the resort and relax. Walk around the bazaar just to feel the hill station. Overnight stay at Periyar.

Day 05 : Periyar


Post breakfast Evening boat ride in the Periyar Lake to watch the wild animals in the reserve and in the afternoon spice plantation tour and evening witness Kathakali - the traditional art form of Kerala. Overnight stay at Periyar.

Day 06 : Periyar - Kumarakom (130kms/4hrs)


Post breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Kumarakom, a beautiful backwater village in the shores of the enchanting Vembanad Lake, a place full of low-lying palms and paddy fields. On arrival, embark your traditional thatch roofed houseboat "Kettuvallam" by 12noon to Alleppey. These houseboats are equipped with all modern conveniences and comforts to make your stay an experience of the lifetime. Journey through the backwaters you can see the day to day life of the local people and the scenic beauty of the backwaters. Your crew will prepare delicious food on board. Cruising through the backwaters you will be going to Alleppey which is one of the most important tourist centers in the state, with a large network of inland canals earning it the sobriquet "Venice of the east". Overnight stay at the Houseboat.

Day 07 : Alleppey - Cochin (70kms - 1.5hrs)


Post breakfast after a thrilling experience in Houseboat, disembark the Houseboat and drive to Cochin depending upon your departure schedule you will be transferred to Airport / Railway station for your onward journey.
