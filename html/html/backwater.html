Duration : 04 Days / 03 Nights
Places Covered : Cochin - Kumarakom - Alleppey - Cochin

Day 01: Cochin


On arrival at Cochin Airport / Railway station you will met our tour executive welcomes you and assist you further transfer to hotel. Overnight stay at Cochin

Day 02: Cochin - Kumarakom (90kms - 2hrs)


Post breakfast city tour, which includes Fort Cochin, Mattancherry palace, Jewish Synagogue and Chinese fishing net. Later drive to Kumarakom, Kumarakom is famous for Backwater and Bird sanctuary situated at the Vembanad Lake. Upon arrival check in Resort, Rest the day for your personal activities, overnight at Kumarakom

Day 03: Kumarakom - Alleppey


After breakfast transfer to houseboat, embark the houseboat by 12noon. Cruise through the back water of Kerala, on the way you can see traditional Kerala village life remind unchanged for many years you also witness the merchants selling vegetables in a boat while the village people await at the bank of Vembanad Lake. Here your crew will prepare delicious Keralan meals from freshly brought vegetables from the market. Overnight at Houseboat

   

Day 04: Alleppey - Cochin (70kms - 1.5hrs)


Post breakfast after a thrilling experience in Houseboat, disembark the Houseboat and drive to Cochin depending upon your departure schedule you will be transferred to Airport / Railway station for your onward journey
